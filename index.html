Babar Azam

Mohammad Babar Azam is a Pakistani international cricketer who captains Pakistan in all formats. A right-handed top-order batsman. He captains Karachi Kings in the PSL and captains Central Punjab in domestic cricket. In April 2021, he achieved the number one spot in the ICC rankings for ODI batsmen.
